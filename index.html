About Yoga Day

International Yoga Day, celebrated annually on June 21st, aims to raise awareness worldwide of the many benefits of practicing yoga.

Established by the United Nations in 2014, International Yoga Day recognizes yoga's holistic approach to health and well-being. The theme for 2025 is "Harmony for Humanity," emphasizing yoga's power to unite people across all boundaries.

Yoga is an ancient physical, mental and spiritual practice that originated in India. The word 'yoga' derives from Sanskrit and means to join or to unite, symbolizing the union of body and consciousness. Today it is practiced in various forms around the world and continues to grow in popularity.

On June 21, 2025, millions of people worldwide will participate in yoga sessions, workshops, and celebrations. Join this global movement to experience transformation and connection.

Yoga Styles

Explore the diverse world of yoga and find the style that resonates with your journey.

Hatha Yoga

The foundation of all yoga styles. Hatha focuses on basic postures at a comfortable pace, ideal for beginners to learn poses and breathing techniques.

Best for: Beginners, foundational practice

Vinyasa Yoga

A dynamic practice that links breath with movement. Flowing sequences vary from class to class, offering creativity and fluidity.

Best for: Building strength and flexibility

Ashtanga Yoga

A rigorous style following a specific sequence of postures. Ashtanga is physically demanding and synchronizes breath with progressive series of postures.

Best for: Discipline, endurance, advanced practitioners

Kundalini Yoga

Focuses on awakening energy at the base of the spine through chanting, breathing exercises, and repetitive poses.

Best for: Spiritual awakening, energy work

Benefits of Yoga

Experience transformation in body, mind, and spirit through the power of yoga.

Physical Health

Improves flexibility, balance, and strength. Enhances cardiovascular health and boosts immunity. Reduces chronic pain and injury risk.

Mental Clarity

Reduces stress and anxiety. Improves focus and concentration. Enhances memory and cognitive function. Promotes better sleep quality.

Emotional Balance

Increases self-awareness and mindfulness. Regulates mood and emotional responses. Builds resilience and coping skills. Fosters positive outlook.

Spiritual Growth

Deepens connection with inner self. Enhances sense of purpose and meaning. Fosters compassion and empathy. Cultivates inner peace and harmony.
